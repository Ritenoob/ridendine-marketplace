Ridendine Finance Architecture

Payment Workflow Schematic

A production-oriented view of how Ridendine accepts customer payment as merchant of record, routes Stripe events into order operations, and records finance state through ledger, payout, refund, and reconciliation services.

Merchant ModelRidendine is merchant of record
Primary ProcessorStripe PaymentIntent + webhooks
System of RecordSupabase + ledger_entries
Safety PatternIdempotency, audit, reconciliation
This document reflects current repo wiring from checkout, webhook, ledger, and payout source files. It does not claim production readiness for money movement without the remaining RBAC, replay, and reconciliation tests.
Customer Payment
  • Validates customer, cart, menu prices, promo, risk, and kitchen readiness.
  • Creates the order before initializing Stripe payment.
  • Returns Stripe client secret and authoritative total breakdown.
Stripe Event Intake
  • Verifies webhook signature.
  • Claims event idempotently before processing.
  • Routes payment success, failure, refund, transfer, and payout events.
Finance Ledger
  • Records capture, tax, platform fee, chef payable, driver payable, tips, refunds, and payout debits.
  • Uses idempotency keys to avoid duplicate money entries.

Payout Execution
  • Preview pulls positive balances from platform_accounts.
  • Execution creates payout_runs and payout rows.
  • Ledger records negative payable entries before settlement records are considered safe.
Refund Handling
  • Stripe charge.refunded event enters webhook processing.
  • Engine platform refund handler applies operational state.
  • Ledger reverses chef, driver, and platform allocations.
Required Gates
  • Finance RBAC on every money action.
  • Webhook replay/idempotency tests.
  • Reconciliation and audit logs before production launch.
